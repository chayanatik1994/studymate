# StudyMate

Welcome to **StudyMate**! A platform designed to connect study partners, help users find the best study mates, and enhance the learning experience through collaboration and shared knowledge.

## Live Site URL
[Visit StudyMate Live]()

## Features
1. **User Registration**: Create an account by entering your name, email, and password, or register using your Google account for quick access.
2. **Profile Customization**: Add a profile photo (via URL) and personalize your account to showcase your learning journey.
3. **Search for Partners**: Easily find study partners based on subject preferences, experience levels, and more.
4. **Study Requests**: Send requests to potential study partners and collaborate on learning goals.
5. **Responsive Design**: Fully responsive interface, ensuring a smooth experience on desktop, table.

Technologies Used

React.js for building the frontend.

Firebase for user authentication.

MongoDB Atlas for database management.

TailwindCSS for styling and responsive design.
